  • Save Myself


    choreographed/led by e.w.; danced by e.w., C.Shetler, H.Yang, I.Szekeres, J.Ho, Y.Teng, S.Sahlool, S.Hong
    We’ve all lost ourselves at some point in life while giving too much to others. But we deserve more love, appreciation, and time from ourselves. So before you save someone else, save yourself because you matter.
    Dec. 2019
  • Coexistence


    choreographed/led by e.w.; danced by e.w. and members of Ecoherence and Attitude
    Nature, in the form of earth, water, and fire, gave birth to humanity. Between mankind and nature, there were only unity, harmony, and peace. As time passed and as humanity’s wants grew, mankind’s collective disrespect and greed towards nature caused nature to degrade and loose control. Mankind and nature are meant to coexist in peace.
    This performance focuses on how humanity needs to rebuild burnt bridges in order to coexist in the face of the climate crisis.
    Feb. 2020
  • Ananke: An Interactive Performance


    Creative Technologists: Steven Wyks & Maria Paula
    choreographed/danced by e.w.
    Ananke, a reference to the greek personification of inevitability, compulsion, and necessity, is an interactive performance that deals with the double-edged nature of technology, in the midst of a society where the lines between technology, surveillance, and data privacy get increasingly blurred.
    Dec. 2019
